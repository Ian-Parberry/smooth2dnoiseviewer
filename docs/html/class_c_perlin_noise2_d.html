CPerlinNoise2D::CPerlinNoise2D ( size_t  n)

The constructor initializes the size, which must be a power of 2, and the mask, which must be a consecutive sequence of 1 bits that is one less than the size. It also creates space for the permutation and initializes it to a pseudorandom permutation.

Parameters
nNumber of consecutive 1 bits in the mask.
